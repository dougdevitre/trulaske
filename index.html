The Future of Trulaske

by Doug Devitre

In order to meet the ever changing demands for students enrolled in higher learning, it's critical that the University of Missouri's Trulaske College of Business focus their efforts on these three pillars.

These include:

  • Transformative technologies
  • Scaling the school to drive higher enrollments.
  • Help students develop an entrepreneurial and intrapreneurial mindset.

Transformative Technologies

It is important to identify which transformative technologies will play a major role in each student's learning and development, how these apply in business, and the benefits they can expect to receive.

Scaling Up

With greater pressure to do more with less, it's critical the university explore ways to scale their programs by implementing new systems, tools, and processes where team members can implement to supplement or replace what they had done previously in their role.

Entrepreneurial Mindset

Businesses today must be creative in their approach to tackle real world problems and it is essential for them to explore how their senior leaders and team members can create pragmatic solutions to serve their customers.

Team Members to ReTool

Each individual at the university will be forced to think and act differently in the context of using transformative technologies, scaling up, and developing an entrepreneurial or intrapreneurial mindset as it relates to helping nuture student development. Please examine each role and how they may be impacted.

Embrace the Future!

In order to embrace the future, the student must learn how to create their own curriculum in a way that is meaningful to them. Learning must be personal, meaningful, and fun!
